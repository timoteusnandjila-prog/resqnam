The Problem

Every Minute Costs Lives

On an ordinary day in Walvis Bay, an ambulance crew stopped outside a home — asking a passerby for directions to a patient's address. The passerby didn't know the street either.

"By the time the ambulance found the house, the patient had passed away. Those lost minutes were the difference between life and death."

This is not an isolated story. Across Namibia, emergency responders navigate by verbal directions from distressed callers. Informal settlements, unmarked streets, and new developments mean responders can spend critical minutes simply trying to locate people who need help.

This problem is completely solvable with technology that already exists in every smartphone. ResQNam is that solution — built for Namibia, by a Namibian.

How It Works

Four Steps.
One Life Saved.

🚨
1. Tap
Open ResQNam and tap the emergency service you need. One button. No forms. No waiting.
📍
2. Located
Your exact GPS coordinates are captured instantly and transmitted to the nearest dispatch centre.
🗺
3. Dispatched
The nearest available unit is assigned. They navigate directly to your GPS pin — no directions needed.
✅
4. Tracked
Watch the responder moving toward you on a live map. Know exactly when help arrives.
Platform Features

Built For Real Emergencies

⚡
One-Tap Request
Emergency requests dispatched in seconds. No multi-step forms. No long calls. Just one tap when it matters most.
📍
Automatic GPS
Your exact location is captured and transmitted automatically. No need to describe where you are or give verbal directions.
🗺
Live Tracking
Watch your responder moving toward you on a real-time map. Know their ETA and feel less alone in a crisis.
📊
Dispatch Dashboard
Emergency services operators manage all incoming requests, assign units and monitor active incidents from a professional web dashboard.
📵
Offline SOS Fallback
No data signal? ResQNam automatically sends an SMS with your last GPS coordinates so help can still find you.
🌍
English & Afrikaans
Launching with full English and Afrikaans language support. Additional Namibian languages will be added after launch.
📋
Incident History
Every emergency is digitally logged with timestamps, GPS data and outcome records — for citizens and emergency services alike.
🔒
Secure & Private
Your data is encrypted and protected. Location is only shared during an active emergency request — never at any other time.
🇳🇦
Built For Namibia
Designed specifically for Namibian roads, communities and emergency services. Not a foreign app adapted for Namibia — built here, for here.
Emergency Services

Every Emergency. One App.

🚑
Ambulance
Medical emergencies, injuries, illness, accidents. Connects directly to the nearest available ambulance unit with your exact GPS location transmitted instantly.

Police
Crime in progress, threats, security emergencies, domestic incidents. NamPol officers dispatched with your pinned location — no verbal directions needed.

Fire Brigade
Fires, gas leaks, structural collapse, hazardous situations. The fire brigade reaches you faster because they navigate directly to your GPS coordinates.

Roadside Assistance
Vehicle breakdowns, flat tyres, accidents on highways and remote roads. Even on the B2 or C14 — ResQNam pinpoints your location so help finds you.
Our Story

Born From a Real Loss

I was standing outside my home in Walvis Bay when an ambulance stopped and asked me for directions to a house nearby. I didn't know the address. They drove on, searching. The patient passed away before they arrived. That day I decided to build something that makes sure it never happens again.
— Timoteus Nandjila · Founder, ResQNam · Walvis Bay, Namibia

ResQNam is a proudly Namibian project. It is funded through the Social Security Commission Development Fund and supported by emergency services across Namibia. Our mission is simple — no Namibian should die because help couldn't find them.
